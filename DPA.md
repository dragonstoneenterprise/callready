# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

**Earnings Pipeline Data Processing Agreement**

**Effective Date:** March 2025

**This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") is incorporated into the Terms of Service and applies where Earnings Pipeline processes Personal Data on behalf of Customer under applicable data protection laws.**

> **DISCLAIMER:** This document is a template and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before use. It does not constitute legal advice and may not be appropriate for your specific circumstances or jurisdiction. This DPA is designed to support compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and similar data protection frameworks.

## 1. Definitions

### 1.1 Key Terms

**"Applicable Data Protection Law"** means all laws, regulations, and legal requirements applicable to the processing of Personal Data, including but not limited to:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European Economic Area residents
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)
- Other state and federal privacy laws applicable to the processing

**"Customer"** means the entity that has entered into the Terms of Service with Earnings Pipeline.

**"Customer Data"** means all data, including Personal Data, uploaded by Customer or its authorized users to the Service.

**"Data Controller" or "Controller"** means the natural or legal person, public authority, agency, or other body which, alone or jointly with others, determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data. For purposes of this DPA, **Customer is the Controller** and **Earnings Pipeline is the Processor**.

**"Data Processor" or "Processor"** means the natural or legal person, public authority, agency, or other body which processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller.

**"Data Subject"** means an identified or identifiable natural person to whom Personal Data relates.

**"Personal Data"** means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, including:
- Names, email addresses, and contact information of Customer users
- Employee information contained in uploaded financial documents
- Any other information that can identify an individual

**"Processing"** means any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, dissemination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.

**"Sub-processor"** means any Data Processor engaged by Earnings Pipeline to assist in processing Personal Data under this DPA.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

### 2.1 Data Controller Role: Customer

Customer acts as Data Controller for Personal Data. As Controller, Customer:
- Determines the purposes and means of processing
- Ensures lawful basis exists for processing
- Responds to Data Subject requests
- Maintains accurate records of processing activities
- Provides privacy notices to Data Subjects

### 2.2 Data Processor Role: Earnings Pipeline

Earnings Pipeline acts as Data Processor for Personal Data. As Processor, we:
- Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Customer
- Ensure persons authorized to process Personal Data are bound by confidentiality
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
- Assist Customer in responding to Data Subject requests
- Assist Customer in ensuring security of processing
- Assist Customer in conducting data protection impact assessments
- Notify Customer of any Personal Data breaches
- Make available information necessary for audits
- Delete or return Personal Data at Customer's direction
- Provide records of processing activities

### 2.3 Scope of Processing

This DPA applies to all Personal Data processed by Earnings Pipeline in connection with the provision of the Service.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Security Measures
Earnings Pipeline implements the following technical and organizational measures:

**Encryption:**
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Encrypted backups

**Access Control:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication for employees
- Regular access reviews

**System Security:**
- Firewalls and network segmentation
- DDoS protection
- Regular security updates
- Intrusion detection systems

**Physical Security:**
- Data centers with 24/7 monitoring
- Controlled access procedures
- Redundant power and cooling

### 6.2 Staff Measures
- Security awareness training
- Confidentiality agreements for employees
- Background checks for employees with data access

### 6.3 Security Assessment
Earnings Pipeline will:
- Conduct regular security assessments
- Perform vulnerability testing
- Maintain incident response procedures
- Review and update security measures as needed

## 9. Data Breach Notification

### 9.1 Notification Obligation
Earnings Pipeline will notify Customer without undue delay and in any case within **72 hours** of becoming aware of a Personal Data breach.

### 9.2 Breach Information
Notification will include:
- Nature of the breach
- Categories and approximate number of Data Subjects affected
- Categories and approximate number of records concerned
- Likely consequences of the breach
- Measures taken or proposed to address the breach
- Contact details for more information

### 9.3 Documentation
Earnings Pipeline will maintain records of all Personal Data breaches, including:
- Facts relating to the breach
- Effects of the breach
- Remedial actions taken

## 11. Return and Deletion of Data

### 11.1 Return Upon Termination
Upon termination or expiration of the Service:
- Customer may download any generated scripts within **30 days**
- Earnings Pipeline will provide Customer with the opportunity to retrieve Customer Data

### 11.2 Deletion Requirements
Earnings Pipeline will delete or return all Customer Data (including Personal Data) after the retention period specified in the Privacy Policy, unless:
- Required by law to retain certain data
- Customer requests retention for longer periods

### 11.3 Document Retention Timeline

| Data Type | Retention Period | Post-Retention Action |
|-----------|------------------|----------------------|
| Financial documents | 24 hours after processing | Automatic deletion |
| Generated scripts | 30 days | Deleted after 30 days |
| Account information | Duration of account + 2 years | Deleted per request |
| Processing logs | 90 days | Deleted per retention schedule |

### 11.4 Certification of Deletion
Upon request, Earnings Pipeline will provide written certification that Customer Data has been deleted.

## 12. Audit Rights

### 12.1 Audit Provision
Customer has the right to audit Earnings Pipeline's compliance with this DPA:
- Once per calendar year, or
- More frequently if required by regulatory obligations or if Customer reasonably suspects non-compliance

### 12.2 Audit Scope
Audits may cover:
- Implementation of technical and organizational measures
- Security policies and procedures
- Access controls and staff authorization
- Sub-processor arrangements
- Data retention practices

### 12.3 Audit Process
- **Advance Notice:** 30 days written notice required
- **Costs:** Customer bears costs unless non-compliance is identified
- **Non-compliance:** If non-compliance is found, Earnings Pipeline bears audit costs
- **Confidentiality:** Auditors will sign confidentiality agreements
- **Documentation:** Results shared within 30 days of audit completion

### 12.4 Alternative Evidence
At Earnings Pipeline's discretion, Customer may accept:
- Third-party audit reports (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
- Security certifications
- Third-party penetration test results
- Other reasonable documentation of compliance
