## 1. Introduction

Earnings Pipeline ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SaaS platform that generates earnings call scripts from financial documents.

By using Earnings Pipeline, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 4. Data Processing

### 4.1 AI Script Generation
Your financial documents are processed using AI technology via OpenRouter API to generate earnings call scripts. This processing is automated and necessary for providing our core service.

### 4.2 Processing Location
Data is processed on servers operated by our hosting provider (Vercel) and our AI service provider (OpenRouter). These servers are located in the United States.

### 4.3 Legal Basis for Processing
For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process data based on:
- **Legitimate interests:** Providing our SaaS service
- **Contractual necessity:** Fulfilling our service agreement
- **Consent:** Marketing communications (optional)

---

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Financial Documents
- **Processing Phase:** Documents are held in active processing status during your session
- **Post-Processing:** Documents are automatically deleted within **24 hours** of processing completion
- **Generated Scripts:** Scripts are retained for **30 days** to allow download, then deleted

### 5.2 Account Data
- Retained for the duration of your account plus 2 years for legal compliance
- Deleted upon account termination (see Section 5.4)

### 5.3 Backup Retention
- Automated backups retain data for **7 days**
- Backups are encrypted and stored separately

### 5.4 Deletion Requests
You may request deletion of your documents at any time by:
1. Using the delete function in the platform
2. Contacting privacy@earningspipeline.com
3. Requesting account deletion

**All data deletion is completed within 30 days of request.**
